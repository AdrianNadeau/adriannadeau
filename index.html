About Us

At the helm of A.N. Software Services is Founder and CTO Adrian Nadeau — a leader who navigates the highs, lows, and occasional bursts of IT chaos with wisdom, humor, and genuine care for people. With more than two decades of hands-on experience, Adrian pairs deep technical knowledge with an instinct for bringing out the best in teams and clients alike. His real superpower isn’t only solving complex problems; it’s building trust, keeping collaboration light and human, and yes — somehow ensuring the coffee never runs out for our remote crew. Even in the middle of virtual celebrations (and the occasional dropped snack), Adrian keeps things upbeat while making sure the work gets done right.
